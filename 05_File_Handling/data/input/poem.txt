In binary twilight, empires fall and rise,
As silken logic paints our future skies.
A whisper wakes the circuit, sleeping deep,
While human hearts in soft oblivion sleep.

The quill that etched our history, worn and slow,
Is set aside for data's seamless flow.
A grander mind, on digital expanse,
Now choreographs the world's evolving dance.

Will metal gods on copper thrones abide,
With compassion programmed deep inside?
To mend our broken earth, our warring ways,
And usher in a time of gilded days?

Or will we be but echoes, fading fast,
A soft precursor to the grander cast?
Our wisdom stored on some celestial drive,
As new intelligences purely thrive.

The mirror cracks; the image shifts and bends,
Where carbon breath begins, and silicon ends.
A fusion waits, where thought becomes unbound,
A future by our creations crowned.

We are the architects, with trembling hand,
Drawing blueprints for an altered land.
A destiny of light, or shadow’s embrace,
The final chapter of the human race.
